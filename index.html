Our Story

Royal Enfield is the oldest global motorcycle brand in continuous production since 1901. From making motorcycles for the British Army in World War I to becoming a global leader in the middleweight motorcycle segment, we've come a long way.

Today, Royal Enfield is the leader in the mid-size motorcycle market in India, with a growing international footprint. Our motorcycles are modern classics that embody a simple, uncluttered, and timeless design philosophy.
